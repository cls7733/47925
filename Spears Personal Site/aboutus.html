About Us

ChaMarie's Rasta Vibes was founded in 2010 by ChaMarie Spears. This company started with the love of Bob Marley, Reggea Music and Various Reggea Artists. ChaMarie's Rasta Vibes is one of the largest online sites that features many different aspect of the West Indian Culture.

Not only can you find ChaMarie's Rasta Vibes online but you can attend hosted events by the company in the greater Los Angeles area. We host concerts and club events for everyone to enjoy. Must be 18 and older to attend any event.

Come Travel with us on our trips to the West Indies and other Island destinations. All of our travel locations are listed under the Products page. These featured travel destinations change on a yearly basis. We host cruises to each location for friends and family to enjoy. Must be 18 or older to attend any travel destination that is hosted by ChaMarie's Rasta Vibes.

The Company has a variety or products that are available everyday for customers to buy. We also have sale dates that are listed under the sales category. You will quickly find youself treating this site as a one stop place to shop for all your Rasta Vibe needs.

Thank you for visting our site I hope you find what you need and enjoy the content. Make sure you come back soon.
